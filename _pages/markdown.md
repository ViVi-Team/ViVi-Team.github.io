## Join VietMed Research Team

VietMed is an academic research group focusing on **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, **Vision-Language Models (VLMs)**, and **medical imaging AI** for real-world healthcare applications.  

We welcome motivated students, researchers, and collaborators who are passionate about applying AI to medicine, especially in areas such as Alzheimer’s disease and lung-related conditions.

### Who can apply

We accept:

- Undergraduate students (research assistants, interns)
- Master’s students (thesis-based research)
- PhD candidates
- Visiting scholars
- Engineers or clinicians with AI interests

Preferred backgrounds:

- Machine Learning / Deep Learning  
- Computer Vision / NLP  
- Biomedical Engineering  
- Data Science  
- Medicine or Healthcare  

### Requirements

Applicants should:

- Have strong programming skills (Python / PyTorch)
- Understand basic ML/DL concepts
- Be able to read research papers in English
- Be proactive and self-driven
- Be committed to research ethics and reproducibility

### How to apply

Please email us with:

1. CV or resume  
2. Academic transcript (if student)  
3. GitHub or portfolio (if available)  
4. Short statement of interest (why VietMed + research goals)

Send to: **contact@vietmed.org**

Selected candidates will be invited for a short interview or technical discussion.

## Participate in Research Projects

Team members typically work on:

- Medical image classification & segmentation  
- Radiology report generation  
- Multimodal LLM/VLM systems  
- Clinical decision support tools  
- Self-supervised learning for small medical datasets  
- Trustworthy and explainable AI  

### Typical workflow

1. Literature review  
2. Problem formulation  
3. Dataset preparation  
4. Model development  
5. Experiments & evaluation  
6. Paper writing and publication  

Students are encouraged to contribute to both **engineering implementation** and **academic publications**.

## How to Write an AI Research Paper

Publishing is a core part of our research culture. Below is a general guideline for writing a high-quality AI paper.

### Step 1 – Identify a clear problem

- Define a real medical need  
- Explain why existing methods are insufficient  
- State your contributions clearly  

### Step 2 – Review related work

- Summarize prior methods  
- Compare strengths and weaknesses  
- Position your work in the literature  

### Step 3 – Methodology

- Describe datasets  
- Explain model architecture  
- Provide training details  
- Ensure reproducibility  

### Step 4 – Experiments

- Use proper baselines  
- Report quantitative metrics  
- Perform ablation studies  
- Include clinical or practical insights  

### Step 5 – Discussion

- Interpret results  
- Explain limitations  
- Suggest future work  
- Consider ethical implications  

### Step 6 – Writing tips

- Be concise and clear  
- Use figures and diagrams  
- Avoid unnecessary complexity  
- Follow conference/journal templates  
- Share code for reproducibility  

Common venues include top AI and medical imaging conferences and journals.
